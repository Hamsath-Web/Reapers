Reaper's Asteroid

Guide the reaper through glowing neon asteroid chaos!

About the Game

REAPER'S ASTEROID is a fast-paced neon-themed action game where you play as a reaper navigating through endless waves of glowing asteroids. Dodge, weave, and survive as the chaos intensifies!

*Controls:* Use the arrow keys or D-pad to move your reaper, avoid asteroids, and try to last as long as possible.
